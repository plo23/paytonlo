    Adobe Portfolio

    Created and completed
    in Fall 2020

    Photoshop, Illustrator,
    Indesign, XD

    This Portfolio was created in XD with a potential employer in mind. This is why I included intentional language, such as why the future leads to "This one's on you." Additionally, the animations lead the user on, such as how "History" moves downward guide scrolling to explore more, and find the "Future".

    In the case that any of the content is not being displayed, continue on this page to see my projects in greater detail.

    The Story

    During this project, I focused on getting to grips with the pen tool. On the left is original sketch of Garfield, on the right is the completed Illustrator file.

    The Designer

    This project built off of my previous work and knowledge of Illustrator as I attempted to bring a real-life image of myself to the program. This stretched my previously-learned knowledge and enabled me to tackle some new obstacles, such as emulating life-like qualities including hair, teeth, and other difficult aspects into a vector image.

    The Reality

    This collage was done in Adobe Photoshop to learn how to compile images. I was to combine 8 images and two quotes. I chose to cover Mount Rainier National park. I learned to crop, merge, and fade images to fit them into one image.

    Imagination

    This composite image was done in Photoshop with the intention of learning how to merge images together in a way that appears original. I merged images to create a mixed scene from Jumanji and Jurassic Park. This included cutting images to merge them with the background, as well as adjusting the lighting so that the different objects matched the scene.

    History

    This project centered around creating a personal magazine, or Zine, in InDesign. I wrote some short stories and articles, and formatted them into a 2-page layout. The above images display the front and back covers, followed by each of the three inside folds as they appear in order.
    If you would like to view the PDF version of my Zine, you can access it here.

    The Cut

    Not included in the final portfolio, these tutorials are practice projects that I used to get used to certain programs and functions, such as Photoshop or working with points. Since these projects took less time and were less refined, I thought them less suitable in the portfolio. I display them here as I can show the full process I went through during the season.
